FAQ! We Have Answers for You..

What are the major NCDs?

Most common non communicable diseases can be categorized as cardiovascular diseases (CVD), diabetes mellitus (DM), chronic respiratory diseases (CRD), and cancers (CA).

What are types of specialists related to major NCDs?

Cardiovascular diseases related specialists - Cardiologists, Diabetes mellitus related specialists - Endocrinologists, Chronic respiratory diseases related specialists - Pulmonologists, Cancer related specialists - Oncologists.

Does this app include specialists related to all the diseases?

No, This application includes the specialists related to major non-communicable diseases only.

I am sick. How can I consult a doctor through this app?

First, you can create or login to your account and then visti the hoempage. Enter your symptoms and you will receive the predicted disease. If you already know the disease, then enter the disease and search the most suitable related specialists according to your disease and get the contact details.

Does this app has specialists from all around the world?

Yes, we have a dataset of major non-communicable diseases reolated doctors from all around the world.

How can I manage my chronic condition?

Follow your treatment plan as prescribed by your doctor. Maintain a healthy lifestyle and keep track of your symptoms and report any changes to your doctor.

How do I prepare for my doctor's appointment?

Make a proper, well explained list of all the symptopms you have. Bring a list of past medical test records or prescriptions you have with you.

What should I do if I experience sudden side effects from my medication?

Do not panic. If you have the contact details of your doctor with you, contact him and ask for the advice, Incase you don't have the contact details, search the doctor you consulted through our web application and take the contact details.
